# NorthOps — Website Copy One-Pager

---

## Website Purpose

Convert visitors into discovery calls through clear, technical, outcome-focused copy. Every page communicates what NorthOps is, what it delivers, and how to start — grounded in real capabilities, real pricing, and real people.

---

## Core Messages

1. **Senior engineering. Shipped in days.** — The headline and identity. Two senior engineers, no juniors, no overhead, production-grade work delivered in 3–10 days.
2. **Fixed-price MVPs starting at CAD $3,600.** — Price transparency as a trust signal. Clients know what they're paying before work begins.
3. **No lock-in.** — First engagement is a fixed-price MVP with a simple SOW. Retainers are optional and month-to-month.
4. **Full-stack, one team.** — Backend, frontend, infrastructure, CI/CD, and AI from two senior engineers. No coordination between vendors.
5. **The engineers who scope the work are the engineers who build it.** — No handoffs, no layers, no gap between sales and delivery.

## Voice & Tone Notes

- **Direct.** Lead with the point. No preamble.
- **Technical.** Name the stack, the tool, the pattern. "CI/CD pipeline," not "streamlined workflows."
- **Grounded.** Every claim backed by a real skill, deliverable, price, or timeline.
- **Confident, not loud.** "We build production-grade AI integrations" — not "We're revolutionizing AI."
- **Outcome-focused.** Frame everything as what the client gets.

**Always use:** senior engineer, production-grade, fixed-price MVP, ship/deploy/deliver, pipeline, explicit scope, simple SOW, days not weeks

**Never use:** solutions, synergy, leverage, cutting-edge, world-class, revolutionize, passionate, seamless, end-to-end, trusted partner
