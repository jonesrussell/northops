## How to Start

1. **Free discovery call** (15–30 min) — we understand your problem and confirm fit
2. **Simple SOW** (1–2 pages) — scope, deliverables, timeline, fixed price
3. **50% deposit via e-transfer** — work begins
4. **We deliver** — you review against acceptance criteria
5. **50% on completion** — done

No long contracts for the first engagement. No onboarding overhead. No project managers between you and the engineers.
