## ProcessSteps

### steps[]

1. **Discovery call** — Free 15–30 minute call. Tell us what you need built. We'll tell you if we can help.
2. **Scope** — We define deliverables, timeline, and price. You know exactly what you're getting before any money changes hands.
3. **SOW + payment** — Simple 1–2 page statement of work. 50% upfront via e-transfer. Work begins.
4. **Build** — Senior engineers build. Direct access, daily check-ins if you want them. The engineers who scoped the work are the engineers who build it.
5. **Deliver** — We deploy, you review against acceptance criteria. Remaining 50% on completion. Done.
