## About NorthOps

NorthOps is a two-person senior engineering company based in Northern Ontario. Russell builds backend systems, content pipelines, and deploy automation across Laravel, Drupal, Go, and Rust. Luc builds frontend applications, TypeScript APIs, and AI integrations across React, Rails, and Python.

We started NorthOps because most teams don't need another agency. They need two senior engineers who can look at the system, understand the constraints, and ship the work. No account managers, no project coordinators, no layers between us and the code.

We focus on the work that requires judgement: architecture decisions, production deploys, legacy modernization, and AI integration. Every engagement is scoped, priced, and delivered by the people who do the work.
