# M1: Positioning Statement — NorthOps

> Final positioning statement, supporting narrative, and mapping to service lines and client segments.

## Positioning Statement

**For agencies, startups, and SMBs that need senior engineering without the overhead of a full team, NorthOps is the two-person engineering company that delivers production-grade web applications, pipelines, DevOps, and AI integration in days — because every hour of NorthOps time is a senior engineer building, not a coordinator managing.**

---

## Supporting Narrative

### The Problem

You need senior engineering work done — a Laravel rewrite, a CI/CD pipeline, an AI feature — but hiring full-time is slow, expensive, and overkill for the scope. Agencies hit capacity limits and lose projects. Startups deploy manually and waste hours on broken releases. Product teams want AI features but don't have the in-house expertise to ship them safely. The common thread: you need experienced engineers who can start immediately, deliver fast, and leave your codebase better than they found it.

### How NorthOps Solves It

NorthOps is two senior engineers — Russell and Luc — who cover the full stack without handoff gaps. Russell builds backend systems, content pipelines, and deploy automation across Laravel, Drupal, Go, and Rust. Luc builds frontend applications, TypeScript APIs, and AI integrations across React, Rails, and Python. Together, they deliver across four service lines: Web Application Engineering, Content & Data Pipelines, DevOps & CI/CD, and AI Engineering. Fixed-price MVPs start at CAD $3,600. First engagements close with a simple SOW and e-transfer — no procurement process, no onboarding overhead.

### Why NorthOps Is Different

Every hour you pay for is a senior engineer writing code, not a project manager writing status updates. There are no juniors, no layers, and no coordination tax. NorthOps specializes in the work that requires judgement: architecture decisions, production deploys, legacy modernization, and AI integration. The team covers backend, frontend, infrastructure, and AI — so one engagement replaces what would otherwise require multiple hires or vendors. Delivery is measured in days, not quarters.
