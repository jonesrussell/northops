## Differentiation Framework

### Where NorthOps Wins
- Senior-only, every time — no juniors, no learning curves
- Full-stack coverage without handoffs — backend, frontend, infra, AI from one team
- Days, not weeks — MVPs scoped at 3–10 days
- No overhead tax — direct engineer access, no PMs or account managers
- AI capability built in — production-grade, not experimental
- Low-friction first engagement — simple SOW + e-transfer
- No lock-in — fixed-price MVPs and month-to-month retainers

### Where NorthOps Does Not Compete
- Not a full-service agency (no branding, design, SEO)
- Not a staff augmentation firm (no 6–12 month placements)
- Does not compete on price (senior rates reflect expertise)
- Does not do speculative AI R&D (production integration only)
