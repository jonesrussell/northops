## ProofPoints

### items[]

1. **Senior-only, every time.** Every hour you pay for is a senior engineer writing production code. No juniors, no interns, no supervised work. Clients don't pay for learning curves.

2. **Full-stack coverage without handoffs.** Backend (Laravel, Drupal, Rails, Go, Rust), frontend (React, TypeScript), infrastructure (CI/CD, Docker, servers), and AI (LLM, RAG, agents) — from one two-person team. No coordination between vendors.

3. **Days, not weeks.** MVPs scoped at 3–10 days. Fixed-price engagements with explicit deliverables. Clients see results before most agencies finish their discovery phase.

4. **No overhead tax.** No project managers, account managers, or status meetings. Direct access to the engineers doing the work. The client pays for engineering, not coordination.

5. **AI capability built in.** Production-grade AI integration (RAG, embeddings, agent workflows) is a core service line, not an experiment. Most agencies and freelancers cannot offer this.
