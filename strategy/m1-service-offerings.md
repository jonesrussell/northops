## Assumptions

- All pricing uses CAD $1,200/day base ($150/hr × 8h) with a $120–200/hr range depending on engagement type.
- AI Engineering applies a 30% premium ($1,560–$1,872/day) reflecting specialized skills and market demand.
- "Time to first revenue" assumes warm or semi-warm outreach, not cold inbound. Cold outreach adds 1–2 weeks.
- MVP scopes are intentionally small to close fast. Upsell happens after delivery.
- Both founders are available full-time. If only one founder is available, capacity is halved.
- No subcontracting assumed — all work delivered by Russell or Luc directly.
- Contract and invoicing infrastructure (M4) is a dependency for all paid work but can be fast-tracked with a simple SOW + e-transfer for the first engagement.
