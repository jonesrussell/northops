# NorthOps — Our Story

## The NorthOps Story

NorthOps is a two-person senior engineering company based in Northern Ontario. Russell builds backend systems, content pipelines, and deploy automation across Laravel, Drupal, Go, and Rust. Luc builds frontend applications, TypeScript APIs, and AI integrations across React, Rails, and Python.

We started NorthOps because the options for getting senior engineering work done are bad. Agencies are expensive and layered. Freelancers are fast but unreliable. Hiring takes months. We built the option that doesn't exist yet: two senior engineers, full-stack coverage, no overhead, delivered in days.

## Why It Matters

You need senior engineering done — a backend rewrite, a CI/CD pipeline, an AI feature — and you need it done well. Not by a junior supervised by a senior. Not by an agency that takes six weeks to scope. Not by a freelancer who might disappear mid-project.

NorthOps delivers the work directly. The engineers who scope it are the engineers who build it. Fixed-price MVPs with explicit deliverables. A simple SOW to start. Results in days, not quarters.

## How We Work

1. **Discovery call** (free, 15–30 min) — we understand the problem and confirm fit
2. **Simple SOW** (1–2 pages) — scope, deliverables, timeline, fixed price
3. **50% deposit via e-transfer** — work begins
4. **We deliver** — against explicit acceptance criteria
5. **50% on completion** — done

No long contracts. No procurement. No onboarding. The first engagement can close in days.
